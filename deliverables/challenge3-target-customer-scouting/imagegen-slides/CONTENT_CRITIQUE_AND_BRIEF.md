# Zurich Scout - Content Critique And Imagegen Slide Brief

## Critique Lens

Evaluator: Zurich business and technical hackathon stakeholders deciding whether this is a credible, valuable Challenge 3 solution.

What they likely optimize for:

- Business value against Zurich's white-space growth objective.
- Evidence that the prototype works beyond a pretty lead list.
- Respect for broker ownership, CRM reality, underwriting appetite, and internal account ownership.
- A credible path from hackathon pilot to Zurich's D&B 30,000-company universe.
- A demo artifact that feels easy to understand in minutes.

## Top Acceptance Risks

1. **Looks like a lead-generation demo instead of a Zurich operating model.**  
   Fix: lead with qualification and internal validation gates, not scraping or outreach.

2. **Proof can feel narrow.**  
   Fix: explicitly position the FDIC 70-account run as a pilot proof, then show the scale path to D&B 30k + Zurich internal overlays.

3. **Business value can sound hand-wavy.**  
   Fix: use the defensible focus math: 70 accounts became 18 validation candidates; 52 lower-readiness accounts avoided; 26-52 SME hours redirected.

4. **Technical credibility can drown in workflow detail.**  
   Fix: show a simple staged architecture and validation result, then put deep details in the proof pack.

5. **The brand could imply an official Zurich product.**  
   Fix: use "Zurich Scout" as a prototype product mark by Good Boys, not as Zurich corporate identity.

### Slide 3 - Scale Path: Zurich Data Creates The Moat

Headline: Public scouting becomes defensible when Zurich overlays relationship memory.

Core content:

- Public universe: D&B 30k, filings, websites, news, triggers.
- Zurich overlay: CRM, broker, quote, renewal, loss, appetite.
- Output: account review, broker ask, warm intro hypothesis, CRM task, guarded outreach support.

Message: Generic AI can research companies. Zurich wins when public signals meet proprietary relationship data.

### Technical Summary - Architecture And Controls

Headline: Staged agent workflow, structured output, validation gates.

Core content:

- Define ICP -> source candidates -> triage -> deep research -> quality panel -> buyer map -> pursuit brief.
- Structured JSON + human-readable account review brief.
- Validator result: 70 companies, 304 buyers, 18/50/2 recommendations, 0 issues.
- Controls: public sources, negative evidence, unknowns, no autonomous outreach, Zurich clearance before action.

Message: The system is auditable and conservative by design.
