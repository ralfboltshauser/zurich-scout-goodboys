Zurich Challenge 3 · Target Customer Scouting

Main learning

Zurich does not need more leads. Zurich needs fewer, better-justified pursuit decisions.

PursuitLens turns external market signals into Zurich account review briefs: why this account, why now, which Zurich lines fit, who might influence the conversation, and what Zurich must validate internally before any outreach.

How our approach solves the problem

Scouting with discipline

  1. Look externally for white-space accounts aligned to Zurich appetite.
  2. Qualify fit, timing, evidence, risk flags, and likely entry lines.
  3. Route only the strongest accounts into Zurich CRM, broker, quote, renewal, and loss-history validation.

Final structured artifact passed validation with 0 issues.

Business value

Focus expert time where it can create growth.

The case points to a greater than $200M topline GWP growth opportunity from expanding target coverage. PursuitLens makes that expansion operational by suppressing weak leads and elevating evidence-backed accounts for broker-safe, underwriter-aware review.

Next steps for scaling

Connect Zurich’s private data.

Run PursuitLens on the D&B 30,000-company universe, then overlay existing customer, prior quote, broker owner, renewal date, loss-history flag, relationship owner, and appetite class.

With that integration, PursuitLens can suggest warm intros, broker asks, CRM tasks, and guarded outreach after Zurich confirms account ownership.
