Problem approach & solution design

Not more leads. Better pursuit decisions.

Zurich’s white-space opportunity is not solved by scraping company names. It is solved by deciding which accounts deserve distribution and underwriting attention now.

01 External market signals D&B-style universe, public filings, news, company sites
02 Zurich-fit qualification Appetite, timing, line fit, evidence, risks, unknowns
03 Internal validation gate CRM, prior quote, broker owner, renewal, loss history
Core message Zurich Scout is an explainable account-review workflow, not an automated cold-outreach tool.
